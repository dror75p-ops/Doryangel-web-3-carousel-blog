SMS Consent Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 4, 2026

DoryAngel LLC ("DoryAngel," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to transparent communication. This SMS Consent Policy explains how we use text messaging, what you consent to when you provide your phone number, and how you can manage your preferences.

1. How You Opt In

You provide consent to receive SMS messages from DoryAngel LLC by:

  • Submitting your phone number through any contact or inquiry form on our website.
  • Texting our business number directly to initiate a conversation.
  • Verbally providing your phone number and consent during a phone or in-person consultation.

Your consent is entirely voluntary. You are not required to consent to SMS messages as a condition of receiving our services.

2. Types of Messages We Send

Once you opt in, DoryAngel LLC may send you SMS messages related to:

  • Responses to your inquiry or service request.
  • Appointment confirmations and reminders.
  • Property management updates, maintenance notifications, and lease renewal reminders.
  • Compliance alerts and important service announcements.
  • Follow-up communications regarding a pending proposal or onboarding.

We do not send unsolicited marketing blasts or share your number with third-party marketers.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on your service status and inquiries. Active clients typically receive 2–6 messages per month. You may receive additional messages during time-sensitive events such as maintenance emergencies or lease renewals.

4. Opt-Out Instructions

To stop messages

Reply STOP to any SMS message from us at any time. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

Need help?

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us directly at office@doryangel.com.

5. Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan. DoryAngel LLC does not charge for SMS messages. Contact your carrier for details about your plan's SMS rates.

6. Supported Carriers

Our SMS service is compatible with all major U.S. carriers, including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and others. Carrier support for SMS programs is not guaranteed by all carriers.

7. Privacy of Your Phone Number

Your phone number is used solely to communicate with you directly. We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. For full details on how we protect your personal information, see our Privacy Policy.

8. TCPA Compliance

DoryAngel LLC complies with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and all applicable FCC regulations governing commercial SMS communications. We obtain prior express written consent before sending any marketing text messages.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this SMS Consent Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued receipt of SMS messages after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
