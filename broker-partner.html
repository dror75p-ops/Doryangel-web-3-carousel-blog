The Deal — Plain and Simple

Here's Exactly How This Works

No jargon. No fine print surprises. Just five clear steps — and then you'll know everything you need to decide.

01
You place a tenant — what you already do

When you rent a vacant apartment to a qualified tenant, that unit enters the DoryAngel program. Nothing changes about how you operate as a broker. You do exactly what you've always done.

02
DoryAngel takes over everything else

From that moment on, we handle maintenance requests, vendor dispatch, compliance filings, rent monitoring, owner reporting, and tenant communications. You're not in any of those chains.

03
You earn $50 / unit / month — every month

That's your share — approximately 30% of the total property management fee collected. It's recurring, passive income. A unit you placed two years ago still pays you $50 a month today.

04
Your existing broker commission stays exactly as it is

When you rent an apartment, you earn your broker fee — just as you always have. DoryAngel does not change that. The $50/unit/month is purely additional income layered on top of your existing earnings, not a replacement or a trade-off.

05
One quarterly check-in call. That's it.

Every three months, you join a 30-minute call with your owner client and a DoryAngel representative. We review performance, address any questions, and keep the relationship strong. That is your full operational commitment.
