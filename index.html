DoryAngel manages cooperatives, condominiums, residential rentals, and commercial properties across all five NYC boroughs. We serve individual unit owners, multi-family buildings, and full portfolio investors.
DoryAngel LLC was founded in 2010 and is headquartered in the Bronx with offices throughout the New York City metropolitan area. We have managed over 100 properties across NYC over our 15+ years in business.
Our flat-fee plans start at $99/month and include tenant placement, rent collection, maintenance coordination, and monthly owner statements. Unlike percentage-based firms, your fee never increases as your rent does. No hidden charges, no surprises.
Owner disbursements are processed monthly via direct deposit. You receive a detailed statement showing all income and expenses. Our platform tracks every payment in real time, so you always know where your money is.
We have a vetted network of licensed contractors. Tenants submit maintenance requests online 24/7 through our portal. Our team coordinates, schedules, and oversees all work. Our centralized purchasing power saves owners 15–30% on supplies vs. hiring independently.
